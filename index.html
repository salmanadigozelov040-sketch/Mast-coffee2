Our Coffee

Keeping Toronto caffeinated with passion and precision

49th Parallel Coffee

Based in Vancouver, British Columbia, 49th Parallel endeavours to sustainably source and craft exceptional coffee. A technologically advanced roastery with a science-forward approach to the craft, they positively contribute to the health of local and global ecosystems.

De Mello Coffee

De Mello Coffee is an award-winning roastery specializing in specialty coffee. Their team believes in sourcing the best coffee from around the world and roasting them to perfection. Their passion is evident in everything they do, from meticulous sourcing to artisanal roasting methods.
